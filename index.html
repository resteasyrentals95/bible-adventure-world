★ Bible Adventure World ★

Walk through the greatest stories of the Old Testament!
How to play:
Use the arrow keys (or W A S D) to walk around.
Visit the Garden of Eden, Noah's Ark, the Red Sea, the Valley of Elah, Mount Carmel, and the Lions' Den!
Walk up to a Bible hero and press E to talk with them and ask questions!
Note for parents: every answer in this world comes straight from the Bible, with chapter-and-verse references, so kids can look each story up in their own Bible.
🗺️ Explore the world and find all 8 Bible heroes!
Arrow keys / WASD to walk • E to talk
